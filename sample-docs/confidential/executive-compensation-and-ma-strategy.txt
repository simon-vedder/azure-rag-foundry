SECTION 2 — M&A STRATEGY AND ACTIVE PIPELINE (STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL)
===========================================================================

Strategic Rationale
--------------------
The Board approved a buy-and-build strategy in February 2026. Contoso has
€45M in dry powder (cash reserves earmarked for acquisitions) and an
additional €60M in committed revolving credit facility (Nordea, Handelsbanken).

Priority acquisition criteria:
  - EMEA-based SaaS or services company in adjacent verticals
  - ARR between €5M–€25M
  - EBITDA-positive or clear path to profitability within 12 months of acquisition
  - Synergies with ContosoERP customer base (same ICP)
