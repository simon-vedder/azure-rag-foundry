Contoso Technologies — Security Incident Runbook (CONFIDENTIAL)
===============================================================
Classification: CONFIDENTIAL — Security team and IT Confidential Reader only
Owner: Security Operations (SecOps) | Date: April 2026

This runbook describes how Contoso responds to security incidents, including contact
details and escalation paths. Unauthorised disclosure weakens our defences — treat as
strictly confidential.

1. Severity Definitions
-----------------------
  SEV1  Active breach, data exfiltration, or ransomware. Business-critical.
  SEV2  Confirmed compromise of a single account or host, contained.
  SEV3  Suspicious activity under investigation, no confirmed compromise.

2. Immediate Actions (first 30 minutes)
---------------------------------------
- Declare the incident in #sec-incident (Teams) and page the on-call SecOps lead.
- For SEV1: open a bridge call, assign an Incident Commander, start the incident log.
- Contain first, investigate second: isolate affected hosts via Intune, disable
  compromised accounts in Entra ID, rotate exposed credentials.

4. Credential and Secret Handling
---------------------------------
- Production secrets live in Azure Key Vault; never in code or chat.
- On suspected exposure, rotate immediately and audit access logs for the secret.
- Break-glass admin account credentials are sealed; use requires CISO authorisation and
  triggers an automatic alert to the security distribution list.

5. Forensics and Evidence
-------------------------
- Preserve logs before remediation where feasible (snapshot VMs, export sign-in logs).
- Chain of custody documented in the incident log; do not alter source evidence.

6. Regulatory Notification
--------------------------
- GDPR personal-data breach: assess within 24 hours; notify the supervisory authority
  within 72 hours of becoming aware if there is risk to individuals. Legal leads this.
- Customer notification per contractual SLAs; Comms + Legal approve all external wording.

7. Post-Incident
----------------
- Blameless post-mortem within 5 business days; track remediation items to closure.
- Update detections and this runbook with lessons learned.
