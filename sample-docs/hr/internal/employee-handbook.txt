Contoso Technologies — Employee Handbook (INTERNAL)
====================================================
Classification: INTERNAL — Employees only, do not share externally
Owner: People & Culture | Last updated: May 2026

This handbook expands on the public HR overview with the policies employees are
expected to follow. It is for internal use only.

1. Code of Conduct
------------------
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect; harassment of any kind is
  grounds for disciplinary action up to termination.
- Conflicts of interest (side employment, board seats, supplier relationships) must be
  declared to your manager and HR before they arise.
- Insider information must never be used for personal trading (see the Confidential
  compensation/M&A material restrictions held by Legal).

2. Performance and Reviews
--------------------------
- Two formal review cycles per year: mid-year (June) and annual (December).
- Ratings: Exceeds / Meets / Developing / Below. Below requires a documented
  improvement plan with HR involvement.
- Promotions are calibrated across teams in the December cycle. Managers submit cases
  via the HR portal by November 15.

3. Remote and Hybrid Work
-------------------------
- Anchor days are agreed per team; exceptions for caregiving and medical needs.
- Cross-border remote work beyond 20 working days/year requires HR + Tax review
  (permanent establishment and social security implications).

4. Expenses and Travel
----------------------
- Economy class for flights under 4 hours; premium economy permitted above that.
- Per diems: Sweden €70, Germany €60, UK €75, Singapore €65.
- Submit expenses within 30 days via the finance portal; manager approval required.

5. Leave Details
----------------
- Carryover: up to 5 unused vacation days roll into the next year (Sweden); use-by March 31.
- Sabbatical: eligible after 5 years of service, up to 3 months unpaid, subject to approval.
- Bereavement: up to 5 paid days for immediate family.

6. Leaving Contoso
------------------
- Notice periods follow local law and your contract (typically 1–3 months).
- Offboarding checklist: return devices to IT, knowledge handover, exit interview with HR.
- Stock options follow the rules in your grant agreement; HR Confidential holds the detail.
