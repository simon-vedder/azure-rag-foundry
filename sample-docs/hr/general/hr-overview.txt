Contoso Technologies — HR Overview
===================================
Classification: GENERAL — Visible to all employees

Welcome to the People & Culture team at Contoso Technologies. This overview answers
the most common questions employees have about HR. Anyone in the company can read it.

How to reach HR
---------------
- HR service desk: people@contoso-tech.internal
- HR portal: https://people.contoso-tech.internal
- Walk-in hours: Stockholm HQ, 2nd floor, Tue & Thu 13:00–15:00 CET
- Confidential whistleblowing line: ethics@contoso-tech.internal (handled by external ombudsman)

Working at Contoso
------------------
- Standard week: 40 hours, flexible core hours 10:00–15:00 in your local time zone.
- Hybrid policy: minimum 2 office days per week; teams may agree on fixed anchor days.
- Offices: Stockholm (HQ), Berlin, London, Singapore. Remote-friendly within those countries.

Benefits (summary)
------------------
- 30 days paid annual leave (Sweden); local statutory minimums apply elsewhere.
- Occupational pension via Collectum (Sweden) or local equivalent.
- Wellness allowance: €400/year (gym, equipment, courses).
- Parental leave topped up to 90% of salary for the first 6 months.
- Annual learning budget: €1,500 per employee for courses, books, and conferences.

Time off and leave
------------------
- Request leave in the HR portal at least 2 weeks ahead for planned absence.
- Sick leave: notify your manager and log it in the portal the same day.
- Public holidays follow the calendar of your office location.

Onboarding
----------
New joiners get a 30-60-90 day plan, a buddy, and an IT starter kit (see the IT topic
for device setup). Your manager schedules a probation review at the 90-day mark.

For internal policy detail (the full employee handbook) you need the HR Internal Reader
role. Compensation banding is restricted to the HR Confidential Reader role.
